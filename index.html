Ictia Coaching

Helping unique people achieve personal goals

In my youth I have competed in both road and cyclocross events, ran marathons, and completed full triathlons. Then life happend, and I became fat, lazy, and out-of-shape. In 2013 I was diagnosed with testicular cancer and this showed me that it was time for a complete lifestyle makeover. I revived my old love for long-distance cycling, and made health my personal goal. Ditching bad habits and creating good ones has changed my life's course 180 degrees. If you too want to turn your life around, then let my experience guide you. If you are ready to dust off that old bicycle and get moving again, then have a look at the services that I offer and contact me.
