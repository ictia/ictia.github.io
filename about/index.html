About

Ictia Cycling is a cycling coaching company that helps people develop themselves to be the best they can be. The main focus is on complete beginners and entry-level cyclists. Athletes receive support through one-on-one coaching, educational blogging and focused structured training plans. The mission is to help unique people achieve their personal goals.

Helping unique people achieve personal goals

My story Link to heading

I have been a cyclist whole my life. In my youth I have competed in both road and cyclocross events, ran marathons, and completed full triathlons. Then life happened, and I became fat, lazy, and out-of-shape.

In 2013 I was diagnosed with testicular cancer and this dramatic turn of events made me see that it was time for a complete make-over - it was literally a life-changing moment. It made me realize that I needed to change my set ways and turn my life completely around, to do anything I could to be there for my daughter for as long as possible.

I took action and did turn my life around. I rekindled and reconnected with my old love for endurance sports, and cycling in particular. My health improved and in 2019 I cycled 2000km from Finland to the Netherlands.

I vowed as my personal goal to remain healthy and active, even into extreme old age. Ditching bad habits and creating good ones has changed my life’s course 180 degrees.

I was fat, lazy, and out-of-shape. Then cancer made me see life in a whole new way.

My personal mission Link to heading

I want to help you to get moving again.

I want to help you to get out of that sedentary lifestyle.

Those who don’t know where to start, those who need that extra motivational push, those who need a bit of guidance into the right direction… Those are the ones who can benefit from my help.

If you think you are lazy, if you think that you can’t do it, if you don’t know how to start, if you think that you are too old, if you are dealing with the problems of cancer, or dealing with other problems of life, then know that you can overcome all that and more, by the simple acts of goal-setting, habit-forming, and cycling. Cycling is a gratifying way to see life’s larger picture.

Your health is not a subject for procrastination
