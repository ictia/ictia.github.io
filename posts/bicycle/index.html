Why the bicycle?

April 9, 2022 One-minute read
Cycling

I come from a cycling nation, so the bicycle is always high on my mind.

Compared to running, cycling has far less impact on your joints, namely knees and hips, it feels less strenuous yet is a great vehicle for improving your health.

Cycling relaxes my mind, after a while, and certainly out in the countryside, it wanders off into all kinds of new and interesting directions. A good exercise brings peace of mind.

Last but not least, it’s a sport you can do sitting down :).
